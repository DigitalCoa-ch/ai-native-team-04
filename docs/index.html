AI Construction Monitoring System

Real-time coordination error detection for large-scale construction projects

System Overview

Analyzes all construction data in real time and reconstructs the actual progress of the site. Detects errors, missing information, and coordination conflicts across different teams (plumbing, electrical, structure, safety) and sends alerts ranked by severity.

Intended Users

Site Managers

Senior on-site leaders who need holistic project visibility and critical alert prioritization.

Executive Dashboard

Project Supervisors

Day-to-day coordinators managing multiple work areas and cross-team coordination.

Progress Tracking

Construction Foremen

Trade team leaders who need simplified updates and early blocker warnings.

Mobile Interface

Engineering Coordinators

Technical specialists ensuring design alignment and detecting clashes.

BIM & Design

Core AI Logic

Collects real-time data (photos, notes, reports, plans) → Organizes and structures all information → Cross-references updates from different teams → Compares real progress with project plans (BIM/models) → Detects coordination errors, missing steps, and safety risks → Ranks alerts by severity (low to critical) → Sends real-time notifications.

Human-in-the-Loop

The AI detects and generates alerts. Site managers review and verify critical alerts. Humans make final decisions before actions are taken. This prevents false alarms and ensures safety and trust.
